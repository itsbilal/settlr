About


Moving to another city is no easy feat. In a city of unfamiliar neighbourhoods, it's tough to find the optimal region to live in without interviewing to countless people and making numerous countless trips to the area. At settlr, we're passionate about giving you the right decision-making tools to truly settle down.

The data used by settlr is gathered from open data sources such as the City of Toronto Open Data Catalogue as well as data from Walkscore.com.

settlr was designed and created by Adrian, Bilal, Tim, and Yuting at MHacks:Refactor 2016 and was a Top 10 finalist along with winning the prize for the Best Use of Esri APIs.
